As Seen

  • Feminist - 2023

    In February 2023, Nikita won a competition in New York City to be featured in a leading Brooklyn-born publication 'FEMINIST ZINE'. Nikita produced a figurative painting to explore the identity of the modern, urban woman.

    Feminism is about scrapping body ideals. It is an accepting world that does not misrepresent or pertain to racial oppression, religious oppression, sexual orientation oppression, or any other type of oppression that seeks to define how a woman may behave or how a woman may look. It is the idea that women are truly free of all misrepresentations that surround them. They are complex beings and their existence is not merely singular. They are the formulation of many identities which exist in unity. ‘Fearless Freida in the Flesh’ is a piece that tells delivers this narrative. Freida is bold, she alludes to masculinity whilst being simultaneously feminine. She is fierce and does not conform to the ideal body image inferred by Western culture.
